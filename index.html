LOT 02 / THE DEADLINE

Compliance is becoming a market-access question

EU · DPP

Digital Product Passport

Under the EU's Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation, product passports are being phased in category by category, with textiles among the priority categories expected in the 2027–2028 window. Garments sold in the EU will need structured, verifiable product data. Source: European Commission, implementing the ESPR

US · UFLPA

Detentions are real

The Uyghur Forced Labor Prevention Act puts the burden of proof on the importer. Shipments get detained at US ports until the cotton's origin can be documented — and your buyer's problem becomes your problem within a day. Source: CBP UFLPA enforcement statistics

BUYERS · AUDITS

The questionnaires are already here

European and American buyers are sending supplier data requests years ahead of the regulation, because their compliance teams need lead time. Houses that answer in days, with documents, keep the order book.
